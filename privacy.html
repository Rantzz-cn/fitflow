Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

FitFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness tracking application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Health & Fitness Data: Weight, food logs, daily check-ins, and progress photos that you voluntarily enter
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (features used, preferences)
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type for optimization purposes

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the FitFlow service
  • To track your fitness progress and display statistics
  • To personalize your experience and recommendations
  • To send you reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • To improve our app and user experience

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. Your health information stays private.

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Data isolation - only you can access your data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Export all your data at any time
  • Rectification: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Download your data in CSV format

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Cache data for offline functionality
  • Maintain your login session

Children's Privacy

FitFlow is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
