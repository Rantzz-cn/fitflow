Our Mission

FitFlow is designed to help you achieve your fitness goals through simple, intuitive tracking. We believe that small daily habits lead to big transformations.

Features

  • Food & macro tracking with meal categories
  • Weight progress with goal predictions
  • Daily check-ins with streak tracking
  • Progress photos with before/after comparison
  • Achievements & gamification
  • Personalized targets based on your BMI
  • Export your data anytime
  • Works offline as a PWA
